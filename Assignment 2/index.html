What is Coronavirus ?

Coronavirus disease is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Majority people infected with the virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring any special treatment.


Coronavirus affects different people in different ways. Majority infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

Most common symptoms are:

  • Diarrhea
  • Difficulty-in-Breathing
  • Dry-Cough
  • Fever
  • Runny-Nose
  • Sore-Throat
  • Tiredness


Are you aware of how many confirmed cases of Coronavirus there are in the world?

Coronavirus spreads mainly between people in close contact with each other. It spreads most easily in crowded settings, closed spaces with poor ventilation or through prolonged contact with an infected person.

The Coronavirus originating from Wuhan, China has spread to many countries in the world.

This visualisation allows us to observe the total confirmed cases of Coronavirus that occured around the world in 2020.


How countries get Coronavirus under control?

If a person have symptoms, they are encouraged to do a Coronavirus test immediately.

Everyone is considered testing before contact with someone at high risk for severe Coronavirus, especially if they are in an area with high Coronavirus Community Level.

This visualisation allows us to observe the number of Coronavirus tests conducted by countries according to samples and cases at May 2020.




Vaccinations against Coronavirus

Getting vaccinations could save your life. Coronavirus vaccines provide strong protection against sickness,hospitalization and death.

There is also some evidence that being vaccinated will make it less probable that you will spread the virus to others, which means getting the vaccination also protects the people around you.

Coronavirus vaccines are known to be highly effective, but some people will still suffer from Coronavirus side effects after vaccination.
